Coffeebean Picking!

How to Play?

  1. Click your left mouse button to move Bear
  2. Whenever Bear touches beans, the beans automatically get picked!
  3. When the number of beans reaches to 500, do SCREEN SHOT
  4. You can exchange 500 beans to 500 LionBear reward points at a LionBear store near you
